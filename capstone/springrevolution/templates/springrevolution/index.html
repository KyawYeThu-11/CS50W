Myanmar Spring Revolution


About the Coup

February 1st is the day when the people of Myanmar began to confront the most radical political shake-up in history as the unlawful coup by the Myanmar military took place with the absurd and unjustifieable reason of electoral fraud in 2020 election. Having seriously adverse experiences with the military regime, such as tyranny and corruption, for past 50 years, millions from all strata of Myanmar society across all different ethnic groups have joined the Civil Disobedience Movement and nationwide protests to firmly resist the coup and to demand for a just, inclusive federal democratic union. In response, the junta has been cruely oppressing revolutionaries with their brute forces, heavy weaponary, and airstrikes, which results in the death toll of 611 and over 2,500 detainees, including Daw Aung San Su Kyi and other memebers of the legitimately elected government, as of April 5. Notwithstanding the promise of the junta that an election will be conducted again within two years, the citizens have no faith into it, which is merely a part of their hoaxes to justify their coup, whatsoever, and know that in any case can the election in question be a fair, and democratic one if any. In fact, the outcome of this spring revolution of Myanmar will determine the destiny of Myanmar, which is also the reason why the people of Myanmar keep courageously resisting despite the abominable cruelty of the junta.

About the Website

The sole and sincere purpose of this website is to contribute in the revolution with the expectation of impacting the society to some extent. In order to implement so, it currently contains two features, Donations Hub and Stay Connected, both of whose functionalities rely on the contribution of users.


Donations Hub




Resisting the dictatorship and supporting the victims of junta's oppressions require continuous financial support. This website, with categories, consolidates scatterd online sources of locations across Myanmar that are in need of our financial helps, which will (hopefully!) facilitate searching for appropriate locations to donate and encourage in contribution.

Stay Connected



How long the revolution will last is opaque to everyone, and it's such an agony for the people of Myanmar to witness or be subjected to junta's brutalities every single day. Therefore, this feature of sending motivational and heartwarming emails to one another will (hopefully!) offer people mental strength, encourage them not to give up, and keep them feel connected.
