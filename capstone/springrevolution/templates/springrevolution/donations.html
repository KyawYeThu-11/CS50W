Donations Hub



Resisting the dictatorship and supporting the victims of junta's oppressions require continuous financial support. This website will, with categories, consolidate scatterd online sources of locations across Myanmar that are in need of our financial helps, and will (hopefully!) facilitate searching for appropriate locations to donate and encourage in contribution.
